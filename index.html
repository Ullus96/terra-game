Gnomes
The gnomes are a race of diminutive and highly intelligent humanoids, known for their mastery of technology. The eccentric, often-brilliant gnomes are one of the most peculiar races of the world. Gnomes are renowned mechanics, engineers, and technicians, widely respected for their knowledge of the scientific facets of the world and their ability to transform said knowledge into surprising tools, vehicles, armor, and weapons.
